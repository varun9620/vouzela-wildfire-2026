# Event Timeline — Vouzela Wildfire, July 2026

- **2 July 2026, early hours** — Fire ignites in Tourelhe, Cambra parish, Vouzela municipality (Viseu district). Spreads to Oliveira de Frades, Tondela, and into Águeda (Aveiro district).
- **2 July 2026** — Other fires reported the same period in Barcelos, Cinfães, and Castelo de Paiva. More than 1,100 firefighters deployed at Vouzela.
- **3 July 2026, 10:38 UTC** — Copernicus Sentinel-3 captures a smoke plume from the Vouzela fire drifting westward over the Atlantic, extending approximately 620 km at time of acquisition.
- **5 July 2026** — Portuguese Civil Protection declares the fire under control.
- **6 July 2026** — No active fronts remain; hot spots persist around the perimeter. Fire perimeter estimated at ~60 km.
